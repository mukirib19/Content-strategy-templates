# Content-strategy-templates

A practical collection of content strategy templates built from real freelance and professional experience. Every template in this repository is fully filled out and ready to use as a working reference or adapt for your own projects. There are no empty fields or placeholder instructions — only complete, usable documents.

---

## What is inside this repository

- **content-brief-template.md** — a fully completed content brief for a fictional AI technology company. Covers topic, target audience, tone of voice, word count, key messages, SEO keywords, references, and deadline. Useful as a reference for writers, editors, and content managers who need a clear brief format that actually communicates what a piece of content needs to achieve.

- **editorial-calendar-template.md** — a complete four-week editorial calendar for a fictional AI technology brand. Covers daily content ideas across LinkedIn, Instagram, a company blog, and a weekly email newsletter. Each entry includes the topic, format, platform, and key message. Useful as a planning reference for content strategists and freelance writers managing multi-platform content.

---

## Who this is for

- Freelance writers who want a clear brief format to share with clients
- Content strategists building or refining planning workflows
- Marketing teams setting up a structured content operation for the first time
- Anyone studying content strategy who wants a concrete, fully worked example to learn from
