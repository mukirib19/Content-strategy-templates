## Notes for the content team

- All LinkedIn posts should be written in the first person from the Zara AI founder's voice — direct, practical, and personal. Avoid corporate language.
- Instagram carousels should use the Zara AI brand colors and font system. Each slide should have a maximum of 40 words of body text.
- The blog article published on 10 June is the primary piece of content for the month. All other content should link back to it where relevant.
- The newsletter is the primary conversion channel. Each edition should include at least one direct link to the free trial sign-up page.
- All content should be reviewed against the brand tone guidelines before publishing. If something reads like it was written by an AI and not edited by a human, it should not go out.
